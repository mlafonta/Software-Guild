Welcome to the HERO Tracker

With the rising popularity of superhero movies, there has been a heightened awareness of superheroes in our midst. The frequency of superhero (and supervillain) sightings is increasing at an alarming rate. Given this development, the Hero Education and Relationship Organization (HERO) has created this site to track these sightings. Here you'll be able to keep track of the heroes, their powers, their organizations, and the places they're showing up.
It's your duty to help keep the citizenry informed. Please log any information you have.
